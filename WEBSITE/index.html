What is VocDec?

VocDec is a cry-based non-invasive tool for early screening of autism spectrum disorder (ASD) in newborns

Why?

With the child cry analysis, VocDec can help detect the autistic spectrum disorder on the first days of the infant. Early intervention improves and accelerates the current diagnose pathway significantly by early diagnosis.

Quicker diagnosis

One tap tool for supporting the initial diagnosis and faster intervention

Reducing severity

ASD early detection helps to manage the child development and support with milder lower symptoms

Unique technology

The technology of VocDec is innovative and unique through an algorithm that is based on cry-signals of newborns
